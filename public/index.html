About ITM Group of Institutions

ITM Group of Institutions was established in 1991 by Dr. P.V. Ramana, Founder and Chairman. Today, we offer the highest quality higher and technical education at our Institutions located across India, in various streams including Engineering, Management, Health Sciences, Hotel Management, Culinary Arts, Fashion and Interior Design.

ITM Business School is a flagship campus of ITM Group which offers AICTE approved fulltime Management Programs. ITM Universities in Raipur, Chattisgarh and Vadodara, Gujarat are UGC recognized, and offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Life sciences, Law, Design, Hospitality and Management.

The group has 2 renowned Institutes of Hospitality Management and Culinary Arts in Mumbai, as well as a Design School in Mumbai. ITM Institute of Health Sciences is a pioneering Institution, working closely with the health industry to offer programs in allied health professions.

Agitator Blade

Hydrapulper machine consist of a high strength agitator fixed at the bottom of the pulper that helps to break the paper bales into smaller pieces. When disintegrated pulp is obtained, it is sent for further processing.

Hydrapulper

It is circular tank that contains water in which bales of waste paper are loaded for pulping process. Produced pulp will be used for new paper products

Sheet Press

Which will be used for converting plastic and aluminium into sheet by application of Pressure and Heat. Machine Consist Hydraulic press.

Paper waste Pulp

Waste paper after processed in Hydrapulper machine. It contain fibrous material and debris & impurities are removed. Pulp used to produce paper product

Desktop Injection Moulding machine

It is used to produce small plastic particle. It consist electric drive unit, electric heater and injection moulding die.

3D Printer
