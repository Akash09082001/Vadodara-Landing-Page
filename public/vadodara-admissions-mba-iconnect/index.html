MBA iConnect

The MBA iConnect program is a Master of Business Administration program offered by the ITM Vocational University. This program is designed to provide a flexible and customizable learning experience to professionals who want to enhance their knowledge and skills in business administration.

The MBA iConnect program offers a variety of courses that cover key business topics such as finance, marketing, and HR. In addition to core courses, students can choose from a variety of electives that allow them to tailor their education to their specific career goals and interests.

About Program:

The 2-year MBA program is designed to build deep general management and leadership skills & while setting the foundation for a bright career.
